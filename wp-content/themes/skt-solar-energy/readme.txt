== Description ==

SKT Solar Energy is a green clean energy theme can be used by industries of conservation, eco-friendly, renewable, biofuel electricity, recycle, natural resource, pollution free, water heating, sun, power, geothermal, hydro and wind energy and energy rating companies. RTL friendly, translation ready, WooCommerce and Gutenberg compatible and page builders friendly. It is multipurpose template and comes with a ready to import Elementor template plugin as add on which allows to import 63+ design templates for making use in home and other inner pages. Use it to create any type of business, personal, blog and eCommerce website. It is fast, flexible, simple and fully customizable.
